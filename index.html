Global Out-of-Office Management System

2024 | PwC
Microsoft Lists Power Automate Approval Workflows Cross-Border SharePoint

The Challenge

The organization tracked vacation and out-of-office time using a shared Excel file across teams in the US, Mexico, and Argentina. This resulted in conflicting entries, no accountability, no approval process, and zero audit trail. Different time zones and holiday calendars made coordination nearly impossible.

The Solution

I designed a comprehensive Microsoft Lists database integrated with Power Automate approval workflows that transformed chaos into structured, auditable processes:

  • Centralized Database: Microsoft Lists with custom columns for dates, regions, delegates, and status tracking
  • Approval Workflow: Multi-level approval process with automatic routing based on duration and role
  • Automated Notifications: Email alerts for requests, approvals, rejections, and reminders
  • Delegate Management: Automatic assignment and notification of coverage responsibilities
  • Regional Intelligence: Built-in logic for international holidays and working hours
